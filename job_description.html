Job Description

A Phlebotomist is trained to collect blood from patients for tests, transfusions, or donations. They focus on making the process safe, efficient, and comfortable for patients.

Phlebotomists usually work in hospitals, clinics, labs, or blood banks, following strict guidelines to avoid contamination and ensure accurate results.
